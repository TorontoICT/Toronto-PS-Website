Admission

How to Apply

Thank you for your interest in Toronto Primary School! Our application process is simple and straightforward. You can apply online or download the application form and submit it in person at the school office. We recommend applying as early as possible as spaces are limited.

To begin your application, please click on the button below:

Apply Now

Admission Requirements

To ensure a smooth application process, please prepare the following documents:

  • Certified copy of the learner’s birth certificate
  • Certified copies of both parents’ or legal guardians’ ID documents
  • Proof of residence (e.g., utility bill not older than 3 months)
  • Learner’s most recent school report (if applicable)
  • Immunization records
