About Us

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide a safe and nurturing learning environment that inspires a love of learning and prepares students to be responsible, confident, and compassionate global citizens.

Vision: To be a leading educational institution renowned for academic excellence, innovation, and a strong sense of community.

Our History

Toronto Primary School was founded in 1995 with the goal of providing high-quality education to the community of Soweto. Over the years, we have grown from a small institution to a respected school with a dedicated staff and a rich tradition of academic and extracurricular achievements. We are proud of our history and the role we play in shaping the leaders of tomorrow.

Meet Our Staff

Our team of experienced and passionate educators is committed to the success of every child. Led by our Principal, Ms. Nomusa Dlamini, our staff provides a supportive and stimulating environment where every student can thrive.

School Facilities

We offer state-of-the-art facilities designed to enhance the learning experience, including:

  • Modern classrooms with interactive whiteboards
  • A fully equipped computer lab
  • A well-stocked library
  • Sports fields for soccer, netball, and athletics

Delivering quality primary education in Mankweng, Limpopo.
