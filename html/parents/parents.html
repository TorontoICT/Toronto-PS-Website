School Governing Body (SGB)

The School Governing Body (SGB) plays a crucial role in the effective management and governance of our school. The SGB is composed of parents, educators, non-teaching staff, and the principal. It is responsible for creating and approving the school's mission, vision, and policies. We hold regular SGB meetings and encourage all parents to get involved and provide input.
